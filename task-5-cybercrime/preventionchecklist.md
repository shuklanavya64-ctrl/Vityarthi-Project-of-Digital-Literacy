# Stay Safe Online Checklist 

## Prevention Tips

1. Use strong and unique passwords for your email, college portals,
    social media, and banking apps.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible, especially
    for Gmail, Instagram, UPI apps, and banking accounts.
3. Do not click suspicious links received through email, SMS, WhatsApp,
    Telegram, or Instagram DMs.
4. Always verify the sender before sharing personal information, OTPs,
    or account details.
5. Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security
    vulnerabilities and malware.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into bank accounts, UPI apps,
    or college portals with sensitive information.
7. Never share OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or ATM PIN with anyone, even if they
    claim to be from the bank or customer care.
8. Check UPI requests carefully before approving any payment. Remember:
    receiving money never requires entering your UPI PIN.
9. Scan QR codes only from trusted sources, as fake QR codes can
    redirect payments or lead to fraud.
10. Review bank and UPI transaction alerts regularly so you can quickly
    detect unauthorized activity.
11. Be careful while posting on social media and avoid sharing personal
    details like phone number, address, college ID, travel plans, or
    financial screenshots.
12. Download apps only from official app stores like Google Play Store
    or Apple App Store.



## Reporting Cybercrime in India

If you become a victim of cybercrime, take immediate action:

-   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in\
-   Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
