Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

> Do's

-   Think before you post -- Ensure your content is appropriate and
    respectful.
-   Protect your privacy -- Use strong passwords and adjust privacy
    settings.
-   Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
-   Be respectful and positive in your interactions with others online.
-   Use social media productively -- for learning, networking, and skill
    development.

> Don'ts

-   Don't share personal or sensitive information publicly.
-   Don't engage in cyberbullying or post harmful comments.
-   Don't believe or spread fake news without checking sources.
-   Don't overshare details about your personal life or location.
-   Don't let social media affect your studies or mental health
    negatively.
