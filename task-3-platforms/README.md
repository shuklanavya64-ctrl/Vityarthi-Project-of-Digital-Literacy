Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz

A simple educational activity created using Google Forms to promote awareness of digital literacy, online safety, and responsible internet usage.


 Project Overview

The Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz is designed as an interactive quiz form containing 5 multiple-choice questions.  
It helps users understand essential concepts related to digital literacy and encourages safe and ethical behavior in the digital world.

This activity can be used for:
- Academic submission
- Awareness campaigns
- School/college digital literacy programs
- Beginner-level online safety education



  Objectives

The main objectives of this activity are to:

- Create awareness about digital literacy
- Promote safe and responsible internet use
- Help users identify online risks and threats
- Encourage critical thinking before sharing information online
- Improve understanding of digital citizenship



  Quiz Details

- Platform Used: Google Forms
- Quiz Title: Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz
- Number of Questions: 5
- Question Type: Multiple Choice
- Difficulty Level: Beginner / Awareness Level

  Quiz Questions Included

The quiz contains the following five questions:

1. What does digital literacy mean?**
2. Which of the following is the safest password?
3. What should you do if you receive a suspicious email asking for your password?
4. Which of the following is an example of cyberbullying?
5. Why is it important to verify information before sharing it online?



  Tools & Technologies Used

- Google Forms - for creating and publishing the quiz
- Web Browser - for accessing and sharing the form
- Markdown (`README.md`) - for project documentation



  Learning Outcomes

After completing this quiz, participants will be able to:

- Understand the concept of digital literacy
- Recognize the importance of strong passwords
- Identify and respond appropriately to suspicious emails
- Understand what constitutes cyberbullying
- Verify information before sharing it online



  Educational Importance

In today’s digital era, digital literacy is an essential life skill.  
This quiz serves as a small but effective initiative to educate users about:

- Safe online behavior
- Responsible use of digital platforms
- Awareness of misinformation
- Protection against cyber threats



  Conclusion

The Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz is a beginner-friendly educational activity aimed at building awareness about the responsible and safe use of digital tools and the internet.

It is a practical and interactive way to introduce learners to the basics of digital citizenship and online safety.
